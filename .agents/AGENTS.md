# Project Rules

## AI VISUAL ENHANCEMENT RULE

When creating visuals for project showcase cards, DO NOT generate completely unrelated AI artwork.

The AI-generated visuals must remain faithful to the actual products and screenshots provided.

Use the uploaded screenshots as the primary reference.

Requirements:
- Preserve the actual product identity
- Preserve layouts, branding, colors and visual hierarchy
- Preserve recognizable UI elements
- Preserve logos and product personality
- Preserve the real-world look and purpose of the platform

Visual Style:
Create cinematic anime-inspired illustrations that feel like premium concept art based on the real products.

Examples:
- FyneStudy -> anime-inspired educational dashboard environment based on the actual FyneStudy interface
- WeDrip -> anime-inspired futuristic ecosystem control center based on WeDrip branding and website
- SUHASHI POS -> anime-inspired restaurant operations dashboard inspired by the real POS system
- NEHR -> use logo
- Kaizen Cafe -> anime-inspired cafe atmosphere inspired by the real Kaizen branding and story

Important:
- DO NOT create generic anime images.
- DO NOT create random anime characters.
- DO NOT create fantasy scenes unrelated to the product.
- DO NOT replace the product with artwork.

The final result should feel like: "An artistic cinematic interpretation of the real product." The viewer should immediately recognize which product the image represents.
Think: 70% real product, 30% artistic enhancement. NOT 10% product, 90% random artwork.
Maintain the portfolio's cherry blossom atmosphere, premium aesthetic, soft glow effects and cinematic quality throughout all generated visuals.
